# Lifestyle & Culture animal cleanup — 2026-07-16

## Decision

Lifestyle & Culture is now treated as a human-centered topic: customs,
traditions, food and drink, dress, daily life, beliefs, brands, and related
cultural practices. Animal anatomy, taxonomy, behavior, and species facts
belong under Science & Nature / Zoology.

The topic-specific Lifestyle & Culture Starter pack cannot simply move its 21
animal questions to another topic without becoming internally inconsistent.
Those prompts were therefore replaced in place with human-centered material.
This preserves the pack's 100-question size and its existing difficulty
distribution: five replacements at difficulty 1, one at difficulty 2, six at
difficulty 3, six at difficulty 4, and three at difficulty 5.

Easy Does It is intentionally a mixed-topic pack, so its two genuine animal
questions were reclassified rather than replaced. A paint-mixing question that
still carried stale Animals metadata was corrected separately.

## Content and versioning impact

- No question or pack was added, removed, or moved between packs.
- All 21 Lifestyle & Culture replacements retain their prior difficulty values.
- The three former required-choice animal prompts are now normal typed-recall
  cultural questions.
- When loaded, each effective change creates a new immutable question version.
  Attempts, player feedback, and editorial review remain attached to the exact
  older version they evaluated; unchanged feedback is not deleted.
