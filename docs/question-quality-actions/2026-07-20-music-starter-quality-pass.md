# Music Starter quality pass — 2026-07-20

## Scope and editorial policy

This pass audited all 100 active questions in Music Starter after the pack was
reported as repetitive. The active set contained 56 catalog questions under
**Albums, Songs & Recording History**, with very little theory, jazz, classical,
opera, roots, or world-music coverage.

- All 46 current questions already approved in Question Quality were preserved
  without a content change and retain their approvals.
- All 12 questions marked **Needs revision** and all 42 unreviewed questions
  were reconsidered.
- Fifty-two weak questions were replaced with new facts under new durable
  question identities. This keeps historical attempts and points attached to
  the retired question while the new fact starts with fresh repeat value.
- Two flagged questions retained their facts but received targeted alias,
  difficulty, explanation, and context corrections; as changed versions, they
  correctly return to the unreviewed queue.
- The five SQL-seeded Music Starter questions were included in the coverage
  audit and left unchanged.

## Resulting coverage

The 95 catalog-managed questions now span 15 music subtopics. Album/song recall
dropped from 56 to 24 catalog questions. The five SQL-seeded questions bring
the complete 100-question pack to 24 album/recording, 12 instruments, 9 theory,
9 jazz, 9 performers/bands, 8 world music, 6 classical, 4 opera, 4 blues/soul/R&B,
3 country/folk, 3 hip-hop/rap, 3 pop, 2 musical theatre/soundtracks, 2
composers/songwriters, and 2 rock questions.

The original generated difficulty distribution remains exactly 16 / 16 / 28 /
21 / 14 across difficulties 1–5. One formerly five-star Elton John item moved
to four stars, balanced by moving the specialized isicathamiya item to five.
