# American sports terminology — 2026-07-18

Mindspan's initial beta audience is American. Player-facing catalog copy and
taxonomy therefore use **soccer** for association football and **football** for
the American gridiron game. Formal international terms remain accepted as
answer aliases, and official proper names such as *Alliance of American
Football* remain unchanged.

The confusing Trivia 101 question asking what Americans' football is called in
England was replaced with a straightforward soccer rules question about red
cards. It receives a new question identity, preserving any historical points
and attempts on the retired predecessor.
