# Season-count question cleanup — 2026-07-18

## Decision

Season-count recall is low-value when repeated. Retain at most one such
question in a pack and no more than three in the entire catalog. In this pass,
only the comparatively notable *Stargate SG-1* question remains.
Its difficulty rises from 1 to 2, and its classification now accurately places
it under Science Fiction & Fantasy.

## Replacements

- Replaced the *Stargate Atlantis* season-count question with a broadly
  accessible *The Office* question about Dunder Mifflin.
- Replaced the *Stargate Universe* season-count question in Trivia 101 with a
  classic Monopoly property question, increasing that pack's subject variety.

Both are genuine fact replacements and therefore receive new question
identities. Previously earned points and historical attempts remain attached
to the retired predecessors.
